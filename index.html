Smart Risk Prediction

Our system analyzes key health indicators—like age, blood pressure, diabetes, and BMI—to estimate stroke risk with clarity and speed. Built on evidence-based logic, it offers meaningful insight in minutes, helping users take the first step toward timely care.

Senior-Centered UX/UI

Designed with simplicity and comfort in mind, our interface features large text, calming colors, and clear step-by-step guidance. No confusing jargon—just an experience built to support confidence and ease for older users, every step of the way.

Privacy and Data Security

Your health information stays yours. We use secure, local processing and never collect personal data—so you can assess your risk with full confidence and complete privacy.
